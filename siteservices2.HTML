HTS Site Services Solutions

HTS Site Services can deliver Electrical, Instrumentation and Mechanical installation services. Our time served installation technicians and management team can provide a complete range of packages from a standalone resource to a fully tailored solution covering both electrical and mechanical installations.

E&I Site Services

The company has combined the skills and experiences of Ian, Martin and Colin Hold. Ian Hold has worked within the Petroleum industry for over 45 years mainly for the ExxonMobil Corporation. Martin and Colin Hold are degree qualified engineers and have established themselves over the past 15 years within the Petrochemical, process and manufacturing industries. They have worked for many UK blue chip and independent companies from the “grass roots” of installing instrumentation and electrical systems to engineering and managerial positions.

Quality Systems

HTS GROUP adopts formal quality systems to ensure our clients’ requirements are carried out within an efficient and accurate structure. Our experiences of working on “both sides of the fence” as client, engineering contractor and installation contractor ensures we maintain a customer focused technically rounded service. As a family derived and orientated company our main focus is on developing and maintaining a collaborative support network to deliver our clients’ requirements.

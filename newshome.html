InstMC sponsored golf day

New HTS Vans

Posted on ccccccccc at 12:00 PM

Our Local InstMC Central Northwest section hosted its first golf day sponsored by HTS Engineering Group Ltd. at the beautiful Vale Royal Abbey golf club last week. A great day was had by all, with plenty of smiles and but for some tears of frustration while looking for balls! Our chairman must have booked the sunshine as it was perfect conditions.

Prizes were presented by the InstMC section Chairman David Green, with the Vice Chairman Martin Hold providing training and guidance against the finer points of the game and scoring.

This was the 1st Central Northwest section golf day and following its success we will be looking to repeat in the near future, please get in touch and register your interest with Maria Price-Tabiner. For future events and if you are interested in joining our great community of like minded professionals please visit Institute of Measurement and Control (InstMC) website.
